📊 Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard: Turning customer data into retention insights.
This is Interactive data Visualisation tools built to solve the business problem , main focuses on customer churn retention.

The Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze customer attrition patterns and identify key factors contributing to customer churn. The dashboard provides insights into customer behavior, contract types, tenure, monthly charges, and retention trends, enabling businesses to make data-driven decisions to improve customer retention.

Key Responsibilities

Microsoft Power BI – Dashboard Development & Data Visualization
Power Query – Data Cleaning & Transformation
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – KPI & Measure Creation
Data Modeling – Relationships & Schema Design
Microsoft Excel – Data Source & Preprocessing
Data Visualization – Interactive Charts & Reports
Business Intelligence (BI) – Insight Generation & Decision Support

📂 Data Source

The dataset used in this project is a telecommunications customer dataset containing customer demographics, subscription details, billing information, service usage, and churn status. The data is structured to help analyze customer retention patterns and identify factors contributing to customer attrition.

Dataset Structure

The dataset consists of the following key categories:

Customer Information: Customer ID, Gender, Senior Citizen Status, Partner, Dependents
Account Information: Tenure, Contract Type, Payment Method, Paperless Billing
Service Information: Internet Service, Online Security, Online Backup, Device Protection, Tech Support, Streaming Services
Billing Information: Monthly Charges, Total Charges
Target Variable: Churn (Yes/No)

Data Characteristics
Total Records: 7,043 Customers
Data Type: Structured Tabular Data
Format: CSV / Excel
Granularity: One record per customer
Analysis Focus: Customer Churn Prediction & Retention Analysis

✨ Features & Highlights

1. 🏢 Business Problem

Customer churn is a major challenge for subscription-based businesses, as losing customers directly impacts revenue and growth. The company needed a way to identify churn patterns, understand the factors driving customer attrition, and develop strategies to improve customer retention.

2.🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

The primary objective of this dashboard is to:
Monitor customer churn and retention metrics.
Identify high-risk customer segments.
Analyze the impact of contract types, tenure, and service adoption on churn.
Provide actionable insights to support data-driven retention strategies.

3.📊 Walkthrough of Key Visuals

KPI Overview
Displays Total Customers, Churn Rate, Churned Customers, and Retained Customers, providing a quick snapshot of overall business performance.

Churn by Contract Type
Highlights churn distribution across Month-to-Month, One-Year, and Two-Year contracts to identify which contract categories are most vulnerable to customer loss.

Churn by Tenure
Shows how churn varies across different customer tenure groups, helping identify critical periods where customers are most likely to leave.

Monthly Charges Analysis
Compares monthly charges between churned and retained customers to evaluate the influence of pricing on customer retention.

Service Adoption Analysis
Examines the relationship between additional services such as Online Security and Online Backup and customer churn behavior.

Customer Segmentation
Breaks down churn trends across customer demographics and subscription characteristics to uncover high-risk customer groups.

4.📈 Business Impact & Insights

Identified that Month-to-Month contract customers exhibit the highest churn rates, indicating a need for long-term contract incentives.
Discovered that new customers (0–6 months tenure) are significantly more likely to churn, highlighting the importance of early engagement programs.
Revealed that customers with higher monthly charges are more likely to leave, suggesting opportunities for pricing optimization.
Found that customers using Online Security and Backup services demonstrate better retention, supporting cross-selling strategies.
Enabled stakeholders to make informed decisions through interactive, data-driven insights and retention-focused analytics.
